Puli

Puli is a small-medium breed of Hungarian herding and livestock guarding dog known for its long, corded coat.

  • Species: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Origin: Hungary
  • Temperament: Intelligent, Obedient, Energetic, Loyal, Agile, Faithful

Best known for his long, corded coat resembling dreadlocks, the Puli is a hardworking herding dog and family companion. Energetic and lively, this moplike dog breed hailing from Hungary appears much larger than he is due to that distinctive coat. Thanks to his self-confidence and intelligence, the Puli will have no problem being the center of attention in your home. Read more at Puli.
